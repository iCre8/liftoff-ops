# LiftOff learner attendance and communications notice

Status: Approved by administrator  
Approved: 2026-07-20  
Effective date: On publication before learner outreach activation

## Why you are receiving this notice

LiftOff uses an attendance-support system for Cohort 3. The system helps learners record their program-day check-in and exit ticket, helps staff identify missed attendance actions, and helps the program coordinate timely support.

## Information used by the system

The system may use:

- your organization-provided account and learner identifier;
- program dates and scheduled attendance times;
- morning check-in and exit-ticket submissions and revisions;
- attendance status and staff corrections;
- your approved communication preference;
- support or accommodation requests you choose to submit;
- attendance incidents, staff follow-up status, and limited delivery metadata.

Google Sheets remains the official attendance record. LiftOff stores the related incident, outreach, correction, and audit history in its program database.

## How the information is used

LiftOff uses this information to:

- determine whether a scheduled check-in or exit ticket is complete;
- prepare attendance reminders and support follow-up;
- alert approved staff when human follow-up may be needed;
- allow authorized staff to correct attendance and document outcomes;
- prepare minimized attendance reports; and
- maintain security, troubleshooting, and accountability records.

The system does not make disciplinary, payment, or accommodation decisions automatically. A missing exit ticket does not automatically mark you absent.

## Automated communication window

When automated communication is enabled, attendance-related messages may be sent through your approved program Slack or organization email between approximately **9:25 AM and 4:00 PM Eastern on active program days**. Messages are suppressed on configured blackout days and while an applicable pause or accommodation is active.

## Communication choices and opt-out

You may request to stop automated email, automated Slack messages, or both. Use the **Manage attendance communication preferences** link included in automated email, or submit the authenticated communication-preferences form in LiftOff.

An opt-out:

- stops future automated messages on the selected channel after the request is recorded;
- does not delete or change attendance records;
- does not prevent staff from making necessary human program-contact attempts; and
- may be changed later by submitting a new preference request.

An administrator may also record a request received through an approved program form or organization email. Staff must confirm that suppression is active before automated learner messaging begins or resumes.

## Access and sharing

Access is limited by staff role and assigned cohort. Accommodation details receive narrower access than general attendance operations. LiftOff uses approved service providers for hosting, database storage, organization sign-in, Google Sheets, Slack, and email delivery. These providers receive only the information needed for their configured function.

Attendance CSV exports exclude form responses, support text, accommodation details, and message bodies. Exports still contain a learner reference and must be handled as restricted program data.

## Retention and deletion review

Program records are archived at cohort completion where the system supports archival. An administrator reviews retained learner-related data after three years across all system data categories. Permanent deletion is not automatic: the administrator first checks applicable program needs, audit requirements, and legal or policy holds, and records the decision.

## Corrections, questions, and privacy requests

You may ask staff to correct an attendance record, change communication preferences, or explain information associated with your account. Contact the program administrator through the approved program support channel. Do not include sensitive accommodation details in an ordinary email unless staff specifically direct you to an approved method.
